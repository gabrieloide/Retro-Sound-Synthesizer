## Credits & Attributions

This procedural synthesizer is based on the mathematical architecture of the classic `sfxr` sound generator and its subsequent ports. We gratefully acknowledge the following creators:

*   **Tomas Pettersson (DrPetter)**: Original creator of `sfxr` (C++), the pioneer of mathematical 8-bit procedural sound effect generators.
*   **Thomas Vian**: Creator of `as3sfxr` (ActionScript 3), which introduced modular object-oriented parameter structures.
*   **Zeh Fernando**: Creator of `usfxr` (Unity C#), which ported the synthesis engine to the Unity environment.
*   **Gabriel Oide**: Designer and developer of this optimized, modular local Package Manager version, introducing 2D bilinear morph pads and offline editor-only auditioning workflows.
